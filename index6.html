SDGs in RailNetwork Functions

Function/Algorithm Description Relevant SDGs Justification
addStation() Adds a new station to the network. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Promotes sustainable infrastructure development by adding new nodes to the transport network.
addConnection() Connects two stations with a travel weight (time/distance). SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Enhances connectivity between urban and rural areas, contributing to sustainable cities and communities.
bfsTraversal() Performs a breadth-first traversal starting from a station. SDG 4: Quality Education Could be used for education and exploration of the network or data visualization for learning purposes.
dijkstra() Finds the shortest path between two stations. SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Promotes efficient transportation and sustainable mobility in cities.
floydWarshall() Computes the shortest paths between all pairs of stations. SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Helps optimize urban transport networks, reducing congestion and emissions.
kruskalMST() Finds a minimum spanning tree for the network. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Minimizes resources needed to connect all stations, contributing to efficient infrastructure development.
assignPassengerToStation() Assigns passengers to stations with the least crowd density using AVL trees. SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being Reduces overcrowding, improving safety and health of passengers.
heapSort() Sorts stations based on distance or other criteria. SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production Enables efficient use of resources by optimizing logistics and station operations.
findValidRoute() Checks for direct routes between stations. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Ensures the availability of direct and efficient transport routes.
suggestStations() Suggests stations based on input, aiding user search functionality. SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Improves accessibility and usability of the network for passengers.
displayStations() Displays the list of stations with their details. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Increases transparency and data accessibility for better infrastructure management.
calculateDistanceFromOrigin() Computes distance from origin coordinates. SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Helps optimize station placement and travel planning to enhance transport accessibility.
cleanupAVL() Frees up memory by removing AVL nodes. SDG 13: Climate Action Supports energy efficiency in computing by ensuring sustainable resource management in data structures.
